African Parks.
Welcome To African Parks.

With It's Wildlife and Some Species Found Only in Africa, Africa Has Some Of The Most Amazing National Parks And Zoo Where You Can Enjoy Wildlife in All Safety With Professional Guides.

Egypt


Wadi el Gemal


The Park of Wadi el Gemal is the only one in Egypt, besides the one Ras Muhamed which includes a terrestrial area that extends over a surface of 4770 Sq Km, and a marine area that develops over another 2000 Sq Km, extending over 120 Km of shoreline, often bordered by vast mangrove areas. Egyptian National Parks Include Also:White Desert National Park

Morocco

Desert,Mountains and Ocean

Moroccan National Parks Include :Ifrane National Park, Souss-Massa National Park, Toubkal National Park

Kenya

Kenyan National Parks

Kenya is Known For It's Diversity In Wildlife and have Many National Parks.
Kenyan National Parks Include :Amboseli National Park, Tsavo West National Park, Nairobi National Park Nakuru National Park Watamu Marine National Park

Rwanda

Rwandan National Parks

The Cleanest And The Safest Country Has Some Of The Finest Parks: Volcanoes National Park, Akagera National Park, Nyungwe National Park

South Africa

South African National Parks

South Africa Has Diverse Wildlife and Many National Parks: Kruger National Park, Golden Gate Highlands National Park, Table Mountain National Park.
